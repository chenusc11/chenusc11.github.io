Shuai Chen

I am a 4th year PhD student in Active Vision Laboratory at University of Oxford, supervised by Prof. Victor Prisacariu.

I received my Master and Bachelor in Electrical Engineering at the University of Southern California.

Previously, I was a senior computer vision engineer at Huawei Technologies, where I led several commercially successful computer vision and computational photography projects in Huawei/Honor flagship smartphones.

Recently, I joined Niantic Labs for a research internship, mentored by amazing scientist colleagues Eric Brachmann, Tommaso Cavallari, and Victor Prisacariu.
